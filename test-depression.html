Depression Test

1. How often do you feel down, depressed, or hopeless?

2. How often do you have little interest or pleasure in doing things?

3. How often do you feel tired or have little energy?

4. How often do you have trouble sleeping or sleeping too much?

5. How often do you feel bad about yourself or that you are a failure?

6. How often do you have trouble concentrating on things, such as reading or watching television?

7. How often do you move or speak so slowly that others could have noticed?

8. How often do you feel that you are better off dead or of hurting yourself in some way?

9. How often do you experience physical symptoms like headaches, stomachaches, or back pain without a clear cause?

10. How often do you feel hopeless about the future?
