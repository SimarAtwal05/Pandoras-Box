Anxiety Test

1. How often do you feel nervous, anxious, or on edge?

2. How often do you feel uncontrollable worry?

3. How often do you have trouble relaxing?

4. How often do you feel restless or fidgety?

5. How often do you get easily annoyed or irritable?

6. How often do you feel afraid as if something awful might happen?

7. How often do you feel like your mind is going blank?

8. How often do you have trouble sleeping due to worrying thoughts?

9. How often does your anxiety interfere with your daily responsibilities?

10. How often do you feel like avoiding social situations because of fear?
